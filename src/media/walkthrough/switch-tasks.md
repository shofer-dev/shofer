# Switch Between Tasks

Shofer supports **true parallel tasks** organized in a tree hierarchy, with parent-child ability to communicate and control.

<img src="images/tasks.png" alt="Task Selector" width="280" />

## The Task Selector

The **Task Selector** dropdown lives in the Task Header at the top of the chat. Click it to see all your tasks:

- **Colored dots** show each task's state:
    - **Idle** (gray) — not active
    - **Running** (green) — actively processing
    - **Waiting for Input** (yellow) — needs your approval
    - **Waiting** (blue) — blocked on a subtask
    - **Paused** (orange)
    - **Completed** (green) — with a self-assessed rating:
        - **Poor** — task had significant issues
        - **Well** — acceptable but room for improvement
        - **Excellent** — executed exceptionally
    - **Error** (red)
- **Pin** important tasks to keep them at the top
- **Archive** completed tasks to hide them from the main list
- **Rename** a task by clicking its title
- **Delete** tasks you no longer need
- **Parent-child hierarchy** — subtasks indent under their parent
